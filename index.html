WHAT IS IML ? (In-Mold Labeling)

In-mold labeling (IML) is a labeling method in which a pre-printed decorative film is placed into a mold before the plastic container is shaped into
one mold plastic pellets or resin are heated and placed into the mold where it merges with the label to create a single product.

Step : 2

Step : 4

IML Product

Injection molding- In injecting molding, the design label is placed
into the mold before fusing together with the container. After the
plastic pellets are heated, the resin is injected into the mold where it
seamlessly combines with the label.

SCREEN PRINTING CONTAINERS

Screen printing is one the most common print technology in plastic container decoration. It's a technique used to apply information such as size, weight & volume, brand details, durability & legibility, nutritional details, expiration and production dates, symbols and storage instructions onto the surface of plastic packaging such as bottles, jars, jerrycans and buckets.

BIO DEGRADABLE BAGS​

Biodegradable plastic is an eco-friendly alternative to traditional plastic. It is made from organic materials that can be easily decomposed by bacteria and other natural processes. This type of plastic reduces waste in landfills and oceans, minimizing the impact on the environment.​
